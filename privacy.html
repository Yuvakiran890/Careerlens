Privacy Policy

At CareerLens, your professional information is treated with the highest level of security and confidentiality.

1. Data Collection

We only collect the data necessary to provide our services: your email address (for authentication) and the resumes/job descriptions you upload for analysis.

2. How We Use Data

Your resumes are processed by our AI engine (Gemini) to generate insights. We do not use your personal data for training outside of the immediate analysis context.

3. Storage & Security

We use industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit. CareerLens leverages Supabase for secure cloud storage and authentication management.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize Google Gemini (AI) and YouTube API (Learning Recommendations). These partners follow their own strict privacy standards to ensure your data stays safe.
